Last updated: March 15, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Short version: We collect your email to create your account, your uploaded photos to generate artwork, and basic usage data. We never sell your data. Images you upload are processed by Google's Gemini AI and are not stored by us.

1. Who We Are

Beo AI Tools is operated by Beo Art Studio, a creative studio based in Nigeria. Our tools are available at listentobeo.github.io and related domains.

For privacy questions, contact us at: odekeb9@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account — by email or Google Sign-In — we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if provided via Google)
  • Your profile picture (if provided via Google)

Usage Data

We store in our database:

  • Your credit balance and transaction history
  • Number of generations used
  • Timestamp of your last generation

Images You Upload

Photos you upload to our tools are sent to Google's Gemini AI API for processing. We do not store your uploaded images on our servers. Images are processed in memory, used to generate your artwork, and discarded. The generated artwork is returned directly to your browser.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your credit balance and deduct credits per generation
  • To process payments via Paystack
  • To send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)
  • To improve our tools and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and edge functions (EU servers)
  • Google Gemini AI — image generation and transformation
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in method
  • Paystack — payment processing for credit purchases
  • GitHub Pages / Netlify — website hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Google's and Paystack's privacy policies for how they handle data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on Supabase servers located in the EU (eu-west-2 region). We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days. Usage statistics may be retained in anonymised form.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at odekeb9@gmail.com

8. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. These are essential for the service to function and cannot be disabled. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Beo AI Tools is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
